Median of Two Sorted Arrays

Given two sorted arrays `nums1` and `nums2` of size `m` and `n`, return the median of the two sorted arrays.

Example 1:
Input: nums1 = [1,3], nums2 = [2]
Output: 2.00000
Explanation: The median is 2.0 since [1,2,3] when merged is the sorted array and the middle element is 2.
Example 2:
Input: nums1 = [1,2], nums2 = [3,4]
Output: 2.50000
Explanation: The median is (2 + 3) / 2 = 2.5 since [1,2,3,4] is the merged array.
Constraints:
  • `nums1.length == m`
  • `nums2.length == n`
  • `0 <= m, n <= 1000`
  • `1 <= m + n <= 2000`
  • `nums1` and `nums2` are sorted arrays.
